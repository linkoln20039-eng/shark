About SHARK SPORTING

15+ years of excellence in B2B swimwear manufacturing, serving brands across 50+ countries worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2008 in Dongguan, China, SHARK SPORTING began as a small family-owned factory with a vision to provide global brands with high-quality, competitively-priced swimwear manufacturing services.

Over the past 15 years, we've grown from a 2,000 square meter facility with 50 workers to a 15,000 square meter state-of-the-art manufacturing center employing over 500 skilled professionals.

Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer success has earned us the trust of brands in over 50 countries, from emerging startups to established retail chains.

Today, SHARK SPORTING produces more than 2 million pieces annually, serving as a reliable manufacturing partner for businesses of all sizes seeking premium swimwear at factory-direct pricing.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth and development

2008

Company Founded

SHARK SPORTING established in Dongguan with 50 workers and 2,000 sqm facility, focusing on OEM swimwear production for domestic market.

2012

International Expansion

First international orders from European clients. Expanded to 5,000 sqm and 150 workers. Achieved ISO 9001 certification.

2015

ODM Service Launch

Launched ODM design service with in-house design team. Expanded to 8,000 sqm facility with 300+ workers.

2018

Major Capacity Expansion

Moved to 15,000 sqm facility. Reached 500+ workers and 1M+ annual production capacity. Opened US sales office.

2023

Sustainability Initiative

Launched eco-friendly product line with recycled materials. Achieved OEKO-TEX and GRS certifications. 50+ countries served.
