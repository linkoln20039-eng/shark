Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ is 300 pieces per color/style for bulk orders. For sample orders, we can accept smaller quantities at a higher unit price.

How long does sample development take?

Sample development typically takes 7-10 business days depending on complexity. Sample fees are applicable and are credited on bulk orders.

What are your payment terms?

For first-time clients, we typically require 30-50% deposit and 70-50% balance before shipment. For established clients, we can offer more flexible terms.

Do you offer dropshipping services?

Yes, we offer dropshipping services for eligible partners. Contact us to discuss this option and our requirements.

Can I visit your factory?

Absolutely! We welcome brands to visit our factory. Please schedule an appointment at least 1 week in advance through our contact form or WhatsApp.

What certifications do you have?

We are ISO 9001:2015, OEKO-TEX, BSCI, and GRS certified. We can also provide additional testing reports such as CPSIA, REACH, etc.
